Cabernet Sauvignon 2019
Napa Valley


A bold, full bodied red wine with deep
garnet color and a lingering, velvety finish.
On the nose, it offers blackcurrant,
blackberry, and plum layered with subtle
hints of cedar, tobacco, and dark chocolate
from 18 months of oak aging.


Rosé Spritz


A light and sparkling cocktail that blends
crisp rosé wine with a splash of elderflower
liqueur and a touch of fresh grapefruit juice.
Served over ice and topped with soda
water, it’s finished with a delicate slice of
grapefruit and a sprig of rosemary. A typical
drink for a lady.
