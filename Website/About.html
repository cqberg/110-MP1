Our story


ShotPuno began in 2003, when founder
Clarence Berganio and John Benedick Tulabot restored a forgotten
vineyard in Baguio City. Guided by patience
and tradition, they planted heirloom grapes by hand and embraced small-batch
winemaking — harvest at dawn, gentle
fermentations, and oak aging that lets the
wine speak for itself.


Meet our team

Sebastian Moretti -
Founder & Master Sommelier

A distinguished wine enthusiast from Florence, Sebastian dreamed of creating a winery that blended old-world tradition with modern elegance. Known for his refined taste and leadership, he ensures every wine embodies sophistication and authenticity.

Adrian Velasquez –
Head Winemaker

Raised in a family of vineyard keepers in Spain, Adrian brings passion and precision to the cellar. He is deeply involved in every step of the process, from harvesting to bottling, crafting wines that are bold yet balanced. His motto: “Each bottle should tell the story of the land.”

Elena Reyes -
Brand & Experience Director


With a background in art and design, Elena ensures that every bottle tells a story. She leads the brand’s events, tastings, and online presence, weaving emotion and culture into the business to connect with wine lovers around the world.
